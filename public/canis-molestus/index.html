Canis Molestus

Canis Molestus
Warwick Allen
Saturday, 20 June 2026

There once was a dog with a terrible habit
He'd run off to chase chooks and many a rabbit
He'd return at dawn
Face his master's scorn
But the next chance he'd get, he'd be right back at it
